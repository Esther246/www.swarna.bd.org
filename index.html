Aims and Objectives:

The main objective of Swarna Bangladesh is to cardiac poverty through human resource Development. For this purpose Swarna Bangladesh works for providing occupational skill development training to the rural unemployed people, especially the rural women. It also provides to the trained for women for pursuing income generation activates, through self-employment. Swarna Bangladesh along aims at providing basic (non-formal primary) education to the rural poor, both adult and children as education is fundamental ingredient for human resource development. Swarna Bangladesh also aims at providing primary health care to its target population to convert them into a healthy and active work force to contribute to the national development efforts.

Present activities:

Swarna Bangladesh has a wide range of occupation skill development training Tailoring (children’s garments making), homestead gardening, handicrafts (natshi Kantha,earthen pot and flower vase etc.) making, poultry rearing etc. have been conducted very successfully for a number of years. Hundreds of rural poor women have been trained and now they are pursuing income generation activities and supplementing this family income. Recently Swarna Bangladesh has constructed a semi puce training centre on leading elite’s and social workers of the locality and other areas. There is again a seven-member executive body. The members of the executive body are elected by the members of the general body for a term of 02 years. Chairman is the chief executive and head of the executive body. He is responsible to the executive committee meets at least once in every two-month, if necessary move frequently.

There are executive and staff for each branch of activities who are responsible for proper implementation of their respective programmers and activities and acceptable to the chief executive. There are physicians who render voluntary service in the clinic and are assisted by salaried attendants. There are salaried teacher of the non-formal education to the poor children and adult education to the rural poor target women.

Conclusion:

Swarna Bangladesh is successfully trying to improve the standard of life of the rural poor women of the locality and their children. There is enormous demand in the locality and among the rural poor for the services offers by Swarna Bangladesh. So there is ample scope and need for expansion of the services and activities of Swarna Bangladesh. But Swarna Bangladesh is severally handcrafted by limitation of resources. If Swarna Bangladesh is provided with necessary resources it can expand its services and contribute substantially to the national efforts eradication of poverty through human resource development. Swarna Bangladesh firmly believe that Bangladesh can thrive and become a prosperous country only by conservation its huge man power into real resources by providing them education health services, occupational skill development training.

Education and Training:

Swarna Bangladesh mainly works for the development of primary education. The name of their program is PSU. Swarna Bangladesh is part of PSU. 207 education centers. There is 01 education nurse in each education center. They take classes for 25-30 underprivileged children for 6 hours and 02 hours every week. All these educational centers are located in the backward areas of the country. At present there are about 7160 students under Swarna Bangladesh. It is to be noted that Swarna Bangladesh employs 01 supervisor under the supervision of every 10 educational centers.
* Swarna Bangladesh is not limited to the development of the education sector, it does something to facilitate the mental development of the child. In this case, the education of Swarna Bangladesh is divided into three levels namely: a) Pre-Childhood Development Center. B) Pre-primary education center. C) Primary education center. So far, only 91,600 children have successfully completed primary education from Swarna Bangladesh schools.
* Education is an important sector of any country. Achieving long-term economic prosperity is not possible without the development of this sector. Therefore, Bangladesh has played an important role in the development of our country's education sector through government efforts as well as private initiatives.

Mother and Child health:

Mother and Child health ( Mother and child health protection in Covid-19 situations):Sayeda Anwar, head professor of the children's department at Dhaka Medical College Hospital, said the rate of corona virus infection among children was low. Especially in the age group of 0-9 years, the infection does not go away. Although its effect on children is less, there is no chance to be careless with them. Bacteria other than corona virus should not be forgotten. If germs enter the body of these children, others in the family can also be infected through them. So it is better to avoid risk. Although the risk of corona virus infection is slightly lower in young children, children who are malnourished may have a higher risk. Afroza Qutubi, a professor in the Department of Gynecology and Obstetrics at Sir Salimullah Medical College Hospital, said the risk of contracting the virus was high during pregnancy due to low immunity. Corona virus infections can occur along with other viruses. Mothers who are pregnant and have just given birth (especially within 2 weeks) should be especially careful. For mother and baby Experts advise adhering to the general rules of corona virus prevention. Apart from this, the overall care of mother and child will continue like any other time.
